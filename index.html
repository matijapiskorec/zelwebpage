About us

The mission of the Department is the development of techniques and approaches capable of tackling data and information overload, a common problem for most scientific disciplines and contemporary technology environments. Our techniques and approaches encompass a blend of signal processing and machine learning data mining, with modern programming paradigms, knowledge acquisition and representation technologies. The main application disciplines include biomedical engineering, biology (genomics and proteomics), and intelligent instrumentation and process modeling. Participation in graduate and postgraduate education also plays an important role in our activities.

Research groups

Laboratory for machine learning and knowledge representation

We do research and development of machine learning algorithms and their application in data analysis theory and application of knowledge representation and reasoning algorithms development of numerical algorithms for modeling of complex systems. We deploy and develop novel computational approaches based on modern machine learning paradigms to solve different problems in the area of modern genomics, proteomics, drug-discovery and medicine.

Laboratory for information and signal processing

We develop and implement novel measurement techniques and analysis of different types of data mainly originating from real-life sources. Our research is based on theoretical and experimental investigation, covering all phases of the measurement and analysis processes.
